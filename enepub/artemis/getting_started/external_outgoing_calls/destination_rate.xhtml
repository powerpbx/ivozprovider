Creating a destination rate

Destination rates section is empty by default, as opposed to target patterns section, that has all the 254 countries of the world. The reason is that one destination rate will usually imply lots of pattern per country (GSM networks, especial numbers, mobile numbers, fixed lines, etc.).

In most of the cases, this section data will be imported from CSV provided by your Peering Contracts, but for our test we will create it manually. Check section Destination Rates for more information.
