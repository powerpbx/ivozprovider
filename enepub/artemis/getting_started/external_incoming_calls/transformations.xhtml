Transformations configuration

IvozProvider is designed to provide service anywhere in the planet, not only the original country where the platform is installed.

A very important contept to archive this goal is the numeric transformation, that adapts the different number format systems of the countries of the world defined in E.164 [https://www.itu.int/rec/T-REC-E.164/es] to a neutral format.
