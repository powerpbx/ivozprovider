Residential DDI filters¶

Residential External Filters can be assigned to DDIs to temporary forward calls to an external number.

Filters Configuration¶

This are the configurable settings of Residential external filters:

Name
Name of the filter.
Number
External Destination for this filter.

Attention

Calls forwarded by a filter will keep the original caller identification, adding the fordwarding info in a SIP Diversion header.
